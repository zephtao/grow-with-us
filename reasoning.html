What is this website for?

Time your studying with our website Grow With Us! Between 1 hour intervals of concentrated studying comes a relaxing 20 minutes animation of a growing plant. Research shows that a relaxing break, anywhere between 15-20 minutes, can increase productivity by letting your brain disengage without entirely losing the momentum of your work. For most, the brain can only truly focus for about an hour before needing a break.
